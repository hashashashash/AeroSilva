Our Innovation

Biomimetic Flight Architecture

  • Our drone replicates natural bird- and insect-like flight using a dual flapping-wing system.
  • Through multi-degree-of-freedom motion and passive aerodynamic effects, it achieves silent, agile, and low-disturbance flight—ideal for wildlife documentaries.
  • Unlike propeller drones, the design minimizes downwash and prevents disruption of habitats, nests, or vegetation.

Lightweight Structural System (<23 g Total Mass)

  • The entire airframe—including transmission, wing mounts, battery tray, and fuselage—is engineered around extreme weight reduction.
  • A PLA-carbon-fiber hybrid frame provides strength without bulk, while the simplified linkage and removal of unnecessary servos improve efficiency.
  • This design enables longer endurance, safer operation near animals, and the ability to carry small filming modules.

Flexible High-Efficiency Wing Technology

  • Using a 10 µm Mylar membrane with a 0.7 mm carbon-rod reinforcement, the wings maintain structural stability while enabling passive torsion.
  • This natural twist increases thrust, lowers energy consumption, and stabilizes leading-edge vortices during flapping.
  • Compared to polycarbonate wings, the membrane design reduces mass by an order of magnitude and doubles thrust efficiency.

Quiet, Low-Downwash Motion

  • Flapping flight generates dramatically less noise than electric motors driving propellers.
  • The drone’s acoustic signature blends into natural environments, allowing filmmakers to shoot rare animal behavior without intimidation or escape responses.
  • Low downwash ensures leaves, branches, and dust remain undisturbed—maintaining the authenticity of every shot.

Specifications

  • Total Weight: 23 g
  • Wingspan: 30 cm; Individual wing: 15 cm × 10 cm
  • Wing Material:
    • PET Mylar membrane (~10–12 μm thickness)
    • 0.6 mm carbon fiber rod
  • Flapping Frequency:
    • Nominal: 26 Hz
    • Adjustable range: ~18–26 Hz
  • Flapping Amplitude: Maximum wing stroke angle: ~60°
  • Propulsive Efficiency: 80%
  • Reynolds Number: 5 × 10⁴
  • Payload Capacity: 3-5 g (suitable for micro camera or sensor module)
